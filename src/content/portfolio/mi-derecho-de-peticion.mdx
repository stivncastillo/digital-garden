## Description

MiDerechoDePeticion.com is a platform that facilitates users to create, manage and send petition rights to health entities in Colombia.

## Problem

Most people in Colombia are unaware of the power of the rights of petition and tutelage before the health entities, this leads to the fact that the users of the health entities (EPS) are not attended as the law dictates.
